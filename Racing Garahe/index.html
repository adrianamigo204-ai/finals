About Us

Back in 2021, Racing Garahe started as a glass and aluminum shop owned by Sir DonDon. Sir Gerbis Punzal, who served as main worker there, began offering motorcycle repair services after finishing his work at the glass and aluminum shop. His first customer was Christopher Cruz, son of Sir DonDon, and it was then that he established the brand name Racing Garahe and became its owner. As news spread that Sir Gerbis Punzal is skilled at enhancing and repairing motorcycles, the shop became well-known in the area. Racing Garahe is a workshop focused on improving engine performance and extending the service life of motorcycles. Established in 2021, we have provided repair and maintenance services to our customers, and we take pride in our honesty, fair pricing, and commitment to delivering high-quality service.
